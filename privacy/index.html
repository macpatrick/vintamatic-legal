Vintamatic Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 15, 2026

1. Information We Collect

We don't collect any personal information. Vintamatic has no accounts, no sign-in, and no servers of any kind — it's a fully on-device camera app. There is nothing for us to collect because there's no network connection carrying data away from your phone.

The app requests two permissions, both used only locally on your device:

  • Camera — to show the live viewfinder and capture your photos. Photos are processed entirely on your device and never leave it.
  • Photo Library (Add-Only) — to save your finished prints to your camera roll. Vintamatic can only add photos; it cannot read, browse, or access any existing photos already in your library.

2. How We Use Your Information

Not applicable — we don't have any of your information to use. All image processing (film looks, frame building, exporting) happens locally on your device.

3. Data Storage

Your saved frame styles and app preferences are stored locally on your device only, using Apple's on-device storage frameworks. Nothing is synced to a cloud service or transmitted to us or any third party.

4. Payments

In-app purchases (the one-time unlock and the optional Darkroom frame pack) are processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. Vintamatic never sees, receives, or stores your payment details — Apple handles billing directly. See Apple's Privacy Policy for how Apple handles purchase information.

5. Third-Party Services

None. Vintamatic contains no third-party SDKs, analytics tools, advertising networks, or tracking technology of any kind.

6. Children's Privacy

Vintamatic does not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children, because it does not collect personal information from anyone, period.

7. Your Rights

Because we don't collect or store any personal data on our end, there's nothing for us to give you access to, correct, or delete. Everything you create with Vintamatic stays under your control, on your device.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above.

9. Contact

Questions about this privacy policy can be sent to legal@aperiumai.com.
